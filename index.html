Key Finding
The discursive space where AI is imagined as enabling genuinely transformative sustainability futures, the "AI for the Future" zone, exists theoretically but is largely unoccupied. The technology marketed as sustainability's greatest enabler may be foreclosing the very imagination sustainability demands.
